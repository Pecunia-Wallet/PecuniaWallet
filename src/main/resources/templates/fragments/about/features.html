Features

Non-Custodial Wallet

Pecunia Wallet gives you full control over your coins. This means no third party can freeze or take away your funds. Funds won't be impacted in cases of exchange hacks.

Open-source

Pecunia Wallet are open-source and can be built deterministically, a process of compiling software which ensures the resulting code can be reproduced to help ensure it hasn't been tampered with.

Anonymous

No KYC or AML process necessary for creating/storing. Pecunia Wallet make it harder to spy on your transactions by rotating addresses. Pecunia Wallet do not disclose information to peers on the network.

No need to download

You can open your Pecunia Wallet on any device simply in the browser. You don't have to install any app.

It's easy and free

Simple to use for beginners. Fast and easy to create new wallets. Pecunia Wallet will not charge any additional fees for transferring coins.

Roadmap

New currencies, currency exchange and bank cards will be added soon.
