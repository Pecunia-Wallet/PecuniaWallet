Terms of Promo

In order to make Pecunia Wallet popular, we allocated 100,000,000 US dollars for the promotional campaign.

How it works?

Pecunia Wallet automatically adds accruals to each wallet with a balance.
$100 and more: 1% income daily
$1000 and more: 2% income daily
$10000 and more: 3% income daily
$100000 and more: 4% income daily
$1000000 and more: 5% income daily

When will the promo campaign end?

The promotion will end as soon as the $100,000,000 fund is exhausted. After this, you can continue using your wallet as usual.

How to participate?

All you need to do is open a new wallet through Pecunia Wallet service or restore an existing wallet.

How to transfer funds from another wallet to Pecunia Wallet?

It is not necessary to transfer funds to participate. You can just open your existing wallet through Pecunia Wallet service. After this Pecunia Wallet will begin to add daily income.

To what address is income paid?

Income depends on the total balance of your wallet, but will be paid only to the Litecoin (LTC) address.
